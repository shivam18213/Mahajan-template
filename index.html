ABOUT MAHAJAN OVERSEAS

The production of handspun and handwoven, the first fabric to come out of Mahajan’s manufacturing unit, was a realization of a vision. Since then, there’s been no looking back for Mahajan Overseas. What has not changed is our thrust on quality. And our Personal Responsibility In Delivering Excellence.

It was the visionary Late Mr. M M Mahajan, who established Mahajan Overseas in 1969. The Pioneer of this industry in Panipat, Mr. M M Mahajan was instrumental in fostering the ecosystem that put this town on the world map as a textile export Centre. What started as a handwoven fabric manufacturer, added various categories over the years.
